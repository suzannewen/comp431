CATCH THE GRADE

Rules of the Game

1. There are 5 total levels. Each level will be harder than the next.

2. Move your mouse to control the student. Catch the grades; they will decrease as your time runs down.

3. Avoid the "distractions" and walls. Each time you touch a distraction, 10 seconds will be subtracted from your time.

4. Keep your GPA above 2.0 and finish each level before the time hits zero or else the game will end.
